About Dolphins

The term Dolphin usually refers to the extant families Delphinidae, Platanistidae, Iniidae and Pontoporiidae and the extinct Lipotidae. There are 40 extant species named dolphins.

5 Interresting facts about Dolphins
  1. Nearly 40 species of Doplhins swim the waters of the world.
  2. Dolphins are carnivores.
  3. Known for their playful behavior, dolphins are higly intelligent.
  4. Dolphins are part of the family of whales that includes orcas and pilot whales.
  5. Dolphins are very social, living in groups that hunt and even play together.
